About UseFaircoin Description

This is a directory browser with geolocation to find shops and services accepting Faircoin near you.


You will need to allow access to your GPS to get to get your geolocation. The listings will be ordered by the nearest to you first.


Note that the distance shown on this app is really approximate, since we don't use any 3rd party service for that due to privacy reasons.
To calculate the distance, we use the Haversine formula, which considers a direct line to destination, therefore it will always be less than the distance shown by navigation systems which consider real routes instead of a direct line.


If you are not on our directory yet, you can add your spot using the "+" button on the homepage.
